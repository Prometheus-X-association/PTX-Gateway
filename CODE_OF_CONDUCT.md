# Code of Conduct

This project follows a Code of Conduct aligned with common European open-source and research collaboration standards.

---

## Our Pledge

We aim to foster a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for all contributors and users.

---

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical and project-related matters
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Disruptive or unprofessional conduct

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behaviour, including restricting participation.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

---

## Disclaimer

This Code of Conduct is enforced on a best-effort basis and does not replace institutional or legal frameworks.
